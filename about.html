Hi, I'm Meg!

The Heart Behind Create With Meg

I grew up in Edmonton with a deep love for creativity, community, and kids. For several years I worked at a Child & Family Resource Centre in a variety of roles — my favourite being hands-on with children in Early Start, Head Start, and Youth programs.

I've had the privilege of supporting infants through to junior high students, caregivers, and community groups. That work shaped everything I do today.

I strongly believe that learning through play and sensory exploration is essential for healthy child development — and that creativity is a powerful tool for people of ALL ages.

Now I'm right here in Nanton, running the studio I always dreamed of — a place where imagination is encouraged, self-expression is celebrated, and community grows through making things together.

The Mission

Why Create With Meg Exists

"My mission is to inspire creativity and connection through engaging, hands-on art experiences — creating a space where imagination is encouraged, self-expression is celebrated, and community grows!"

A space where creativity feels natural, connection feels easy, and art becomes a way to express, slow down, and grow together.

Background

What Meg Brings to the Studio

🎓

Early Childhood Education

Extensive training in ECE & Development, including special needs support, cultural diversity, and mental health — with a huge passion for mental health advocacy.

🌿

Holistic Nutrition

Studied Holistic Nutrition and is passionate about nature, biological uniqueness, and mindful living. This shows up in how the studio is run — thoughtful, intentional, whole-person focused.

🎵

Music & Movement

Grew up in musical theatre and loves singing, dancing, and creative expression. Music and movement are at the heart of everything — especially with littles!

♻️

DIY & Sustainability

Crafting, DIY, thrifting, and upcycling are lifelong loves. The studio creates as many materials as possible in-house — better for the planet, better for the process.
